Why Choose Nintendo Switch?

Nintendo Switch offers a unique gaming experience that lets players enjoy their favorite games anytime and anywhere. Whether at home on the TV or on the go in handheld mode, it gives users flexibility and convenience in one console.

With its wide selection of popular games, family-friendly entertainment, and creative play styles, Nintendo Switch continues to be one of the most loved gaming systems for players of all ages.
